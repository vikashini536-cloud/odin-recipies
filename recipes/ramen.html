RAMEN

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

  • Instant ramen noodles
  • Water
  • Ramen seasoning packet
  • Egg
  • cheese slices or shredded cheese
  • Chili oil
  • Chilli flakes
  • Oregano

PROCEDURE BY VIKS

  1. Boil water in a pot and add the ramen noodles carefully.
  2. Cook the noodles until they become soft and fully cooked.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the ramen seasoning with a little hot water.
  4. Drain the noodles lightly while keeping a small amount of broth aside.
  5. Add the noodles to the seasoning mixture and stir everything well.
  6. Crack an egg into the ramen or add boiled egg slices on top.
  7. Sprinkle chili flakes, oregano, or chili oil for extra flavor.
  8. Add cheese, vegetables, or any toppings you like to the ramen.
  9. Serve the ramen hot with your favorite show playing nearby.
