CHOCOLATE CAKE

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

  • 2 1/4 cups (270g) King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon table salt
  • 4 cup (64g) unsweetened cocoa, Dutch-process or natural
  • 1 3/4 cups (354g) granulated sugar
  • tablespoons (113g) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • /3 cup (64g) vegetable oil
  • teaspoons King Arthur Pure Vanilla Extract
  • cup (227g) milk
  • 1/2 cup (113g) brewed coffee or water
  • large eggs

PROCEDURE BY VIKS

  1. Preheat the oven and grease the cake pan with butter or oil.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.
  4. Whisk everything together until the batter becomes smooth and lump-free.
  5. Pour the chocolate batter evenly into the prepared cake pan.
  6. Bake the cake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean
  7. Let the cake cool completely before removing it from the pan.
  8. Spread chocolate frosting evenly over the top and sides of the cake.
  9. Slice the cake, serve it nicely, and enjoy every bite. 🍫✨
